THE MISSION

Free the Data joins a growing movement that calls on academics, journalists, scientists, and anyone else interested in data justice to challenge what it means to work with data. Our goal is to to tell stories that use data in compelling, interactive, and visual ways, while breaking stereotypes about who can do data analysis and what proper research topics look like. Collectively, the content that we showcase reflects the diversity of our interests and our shared commitment to driving data in more ethical, engaging, and empowering directions.
